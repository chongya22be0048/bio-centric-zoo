Aim

What This Framework Is Built To Do

To develop an immersive bio-centric arboreal primate habitat that promotes ecological complexity, competence, environmental choice, and control for diverse natural behaviours.

Framework

Objectives

To develop a tropical rainforest habitat that integrates environmental performance.

Complexity Environmental foundation (climate + function)

To establish a spatial system that enhances ecological agency to exhibit natural behaviours.

Competence + Choice Spatial system (complexity + connectivity)

To design an adaptive habitat that enhances immersive and educational visitor experience.

Control + Variable Experience (enrichment + coexistence)
